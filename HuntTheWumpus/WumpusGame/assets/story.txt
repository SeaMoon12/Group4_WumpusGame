The legends speak of a labyrinth of twenty dark, interconnected caves. Within them lurks a beast of absolute nightmare: The Wumpus.

For generations, brave souls have entered this dark abyss. They come seeking endless gold, eternal glory, and the legendary title of the realm's Greatest Adventurer. You have told the guild you are no different. You let them believe you are here just for the fame and the coin.

But beneath that prideful facade lies a desperate truth.

You are not hunting the Wumpus for a trophy. You are hunting it for what it guards. Deep within the beast's lair lies the Divine Relic—a mythical artifact pulsing with ancient magic, said to cure any sickness known to the mortal world.

It is the only hope left to save your dying sister. The apothecaries have failed, the clerics have given up, but you refuse to let her fade away.

With a heavy heart, a dim lantern, and only a few arrows in your quiver, you step into the freezing darkness of the cavern entrance. The hunt begins now. You will claim the relic, or you will perish in the dark.
